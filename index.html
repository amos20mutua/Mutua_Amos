About Us

Perceiving, conceiving, feeling, and dispensing Christ through music, vibing and grooving in Christ.

Our Mission

Us for Christ Band is a dynamic Christian worship group, dedicated to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ through music. Our members are deeply devoted to sharing God's love and power through the songs we perform. We are not just a band; we are a community of believers who have witnessed His work in our lives and seek to inspire others through our music.

Our History

Founded in 2019, Us for Christ Band has grown rapidly in both size and spirit. What started as a group of passionate musicians coming together for worship, has now turned into a thriving ministry that continues to touch lives. Each day, we strive to grow in faith and music, and soon we will be releasing our very own composed songs!

Our Devoted Members

Though our members are many, we all share the same devotion to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ through music. Every member brings their unique gift and passion to the table, and together, we are building a powerful ministry that reflects the love of Christ. We have all witnessed His transformative power in our lives, and through our music, we aim to help others experience the same.
